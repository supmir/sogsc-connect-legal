Privacy Policy

App: SOGSC Connect  |  Developer: Adijaya Consultech  |  Last updated: 10 May 2026

1. Who we are

SOGSC Connect is the official companion app for the Oil & Gas Services and Equipment Conference and Exhibition (OGSE) Sabah 2026, developed by Adijaya Consultech. Questions about this policy can be sent to iskandar.amir@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

  • Email address — used to sign in via a one-time passcode. Required to use the app.
  • Display name and company/organisation — entered by you on first use. Visible to other attendees in chat and the attendee directory.
  • Private messages — content of 1-on-1 chats between attendees. Stored on our servers and only accessible to the two participants.
  • App interactions — anonymised usage events (e.g. screens viewed, sessions bookmarked) to help us improve the app. This can be disabled by contacting us.

3. What we do not collect

We do not collect your location, contacts, phone number, photos, payment information, or any device identifiers.

4. How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To display your profile to other attendees
  • To deliver and store your private messages
  • To understand how the app is used and fix issues

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is stored on Supabase (supabase.com), our database provider, under a data processing agreement.

6. Data security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS. Access to your data is restricted to authenticated users — you can only read your own profile and the conversations you are a participant in.

7. Data retention

Your data is retained for the duration of the event and a reasonable period afterwards. Anonymised analytics events are automatically deleted within 90 days.

8. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To do so, email iskandar.amir@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request and the email address you used to sign in. We will respond within 30 days.

Full instructions: Account Deletion Request

9. Children

This app is intended for professional conference attendees aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
