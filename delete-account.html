Account Deletion Request

App: SOGSC Connect  |  Developer: Adijaya Consultech

How to request deletion

Send an email to iskandar.amir@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: The email address you used to sign in to SOGSC Connect

We will action your request within 30 days and send a confirmation to the email address provided.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile — display name and company
  • All private messages you have sent or received
  • Your bookmarked sessions

What may be retained

  • Anonymised analytics events (not linked to your identity) may be retained for up to 90 days before automatic deletion.
